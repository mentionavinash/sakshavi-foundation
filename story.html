The Journey Behind Sakshavi Foundation

About Sakshi

Sakshavi Foundation is inspired by Sakshi, whose kindness, compassion, and positive approach towards life became the foundation of this journey. Her values of caring for others, spreading happiness, and believing in meaningful actions continue to guide the mission of Sakshavi Foundation.

About Avi

Avinash Kumar (Avi), Founder, CEO & President of Sakshavi Foundation, started this initiative with the vision of transforming inspiration into social impact. Through Sakshavi Foundation, Avi aims to create opportunities in education, digital empowerment, environmental awareness, and community service.

Founder’s Message
