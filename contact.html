Founder’s Message

Sakshavi Foundation was founded with a vision inspired by Sakshi, whose values of kindness, compassion, care, and positivity continue to guide this journey.

The foundation represents a commitment to transform inspiration into meaningful action by supporting education, empowering communities, encouraging digital awareness, protecting the environment, and serving society.

Every initiative of Sakshavi Foundation carries the belief that small acts of kindness and dedication can create a lasting impact on people's lives.

— Avi (Avinash Kumar)
Founder, CEO & President
